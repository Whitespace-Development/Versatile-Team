About Me

Hi, my name is George, and I started DJing in 1999. My DJ style is all about fun and creating unforgettable memories with live mixes. I’ve rocked weddings, parties, and celebrations from Delaware to Boston, ensuring everyone dances and has a great time. Meeting new people and making a memorable difference in their events is what I enjoy most about DJing. I’ve had the pleasure of DJing with The Sugarhill Gang and at high-profile events like New Year’s Eve in Times Square and for organizations like the Make a Wish Foundation and Meridian Health. I’d love to be a part of your special day, so let your Wedding Adviser know if you’d like to speak with me directly.
